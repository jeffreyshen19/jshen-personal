about

Hey! I'm Jeffrey (he/him). I study computer science and urban planning, with a minor in political science, at MIT. I want to use technology to bring clarity and transparency to politics, and am especially interested in data journalism, city planning, open source software, and civic tech.

I'm currently an undergraduate researcher working on LobbyView, a database of money in U.S. politics, and am also a volunteer data analyst for Sri Kulkarni's 2020 Congressional campaign. I also run a blog and a micro-blog, where I write about data visualization, politics, and more!
